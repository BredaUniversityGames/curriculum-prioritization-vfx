At the CMGT Games course at BUAS, we are conducting a strategic review of our 4-year Bachelor's degree program in Visual Arts. Our goal is to create a curriculum that effectively prepares students for careers in both the Film/VFX and Game development industries. Your expertise is crucial in helping us answer a fundamental question: In a constrained 4-year timeline, which roles, skills, and experiences are most critical for a graduate's employability in your sector? This survey analyzes responses from 119 industry professionals to inform curriculum development and ensure our graduates are well-prepared for the evolving demands of the digital arts industry.
